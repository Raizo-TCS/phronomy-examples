# Refund Policy

## Overview

Acme Corp offers a straightforward refund policy to ensure customer satisfaction.

## Refund Window

Customers may request a full refund within **30 days** of the original purchase date.
Requests submitted after 30 days will not be accepted under standard policy.

## Eligible Conditions

A refund is eligible when:

- The item is unused and in its original packaging.
- The customer provides proof of purchase (order ID or receipt).
- The item is not listed in the non-refundable category (see below).

## Non-Refundable Items

The following items cannot be refunded:

- Digital downloads and software licenses once activated.
- Personalized or custom-made products.
- Perishable goods.

## Refund Process

1. Submit a refund request via the customer portal or email support@acme.example.com.
2. Our team reviews the request within 2 business days.
3. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

## Partial Refunds

Items returned in a used or damaged state may be eligible for a partial refund
at the discretion of our support team.
