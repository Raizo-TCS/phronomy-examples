# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Options

Acme Corp offers three shipping tiers for domestic orders:

| Tier       | Delivery Time        | Cost         |
|------------|----------------------|--------------|
| Standard   | 3–5 business days    | $4.99        |
| Express    | 1–2 business days    | $12.99       |
| Overnight  | Next business day    | $24.99       |

Free standard shipping is available on orders over $50.

## International Shipping

International orders are shipped via our logistics partner and typically arrive
within **7–14 business days**, depending on the destination country.
Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

## Order Cut-Off Time

Orders placed before **2:00 PM local time** on a business day are processed the
same day. Orders placed after the cut-off or on weekends/holidays are processed
the next business day.

## Tracking

A tracking number is emailed within 24 hours of shipment. Customers can track
their order on the carrier's website or through the Acme customer portal.

## Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned as undeliverable, we will contact the customer to
arrange re-shipment. Additional shipping charges may apply.
